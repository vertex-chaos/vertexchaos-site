## Problem

The environment needed to be more predictable and easier to operate without relying on tribal knowledge.

## Approach

- Focus on repeatability and safety (validation, rollbacks, idempotent patterns where possible)
- Improve observability (structured logs, clear outputs, runbooks)
- Keep the solution maintainable (sane defaults, clear docs)

## Result

- Measurable reduction in manual steps
- Clearer operational ownership and documentation
- Better reliability and fewer “surprise” failures
